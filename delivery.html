Delivery & Loyalty experiments

This page compares a basic experience (Version A) with an optimized one (Version B) focused on delivery time slots, substitution choices and loyalty rewards.

Stage 4 · Delivery & use
How customers receive their order and manage substitutions.
Delivery · ETA & time slots

Broad 30–90 min window vs. 1-hour slot with precise ETA

A/B test between a vague “30–90 minutes” promise and a selected 1-hour slot that shows a live ETA (e.g. 18:40–18:55) on the confirmation screen.

A: Broad 30–90 min window, no slot choice.

B: User selects 1-hour slot with precise ETA.

On-time perception +18% Support tickets –12%
Delivery · Substitution workflow

Store decides substitutions vs. customer-controlled rules

Test whether giving customers substitution choices improves satisfaction and reduces refund volume.

A: Store chooses substitutions, customer is only refunded.

B: Customer sets rules (allow / SMS / no substitution) and confirms an example swap.

Order satisfaction +22% Refund rate –9%
Loyalty · Programme design

Flat points system vs. tiered loyalty + UGC rewards

Comparison of a simple points scheme with a tiered programme that rewards reviews and shared recipes.

A: Flat points (1 point / €1, coupon at 100 points).

B: Tiers (Silver / Gold / VIP) + bonus points for reviews and recipes.

Repeat orders +15% UGC submissions +40%

Stage 4 · Delivery & use · Version A

Delivery window and substitutions (baseline)

Version A offers a broad ETA and no control over substitutions. Missing items are refunded automatically.

Estimated delivery: arriving in 30–90 minutes.

If an item is out of stock, the store refunds the difference and chooses any similar product.

  • No choice of delivery slot
  • No substitution preferences

Stage 5 · Loyalty · Version A

Simple points system

A single-layer loyalty scheme where every euro spent generates the same number of points.

How it works

Earn 1 point for every €1 spent.

Every 100 points = €5 coupon on the next order.

All customers are in the same group with no tiers or exclusive benefits.

Example notification

“Thank you for your order. You have earned 42 points.”

Stage 5 · Loyalty · Version B

Tiered rewards & community

A richer loyalty programme with status tiers and rewards for contributions such as reviews and recipes.

Loyalty tiers

  • Silver · 3 orders / month → Free delivery once a month
  • Gold · 6 orders / month → Free delivery + early access to specials
  • VIP · Invite only → Event invitations and exclusive bundles

Community recipes & reviews
