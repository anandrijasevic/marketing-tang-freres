Stage 3 · Decision & purchase
How checkout structure and account requirements affect completion and first-time conversion.
CHECKOUT FLOW

Four-step checkout vs. streamlined three-step flow

A: Cart → Log in → Address → Payment (account required before address).
B: Cart & slot → Details → Payment (account optional).

Checkout completion +18% Time to complete –25%
ACCOUNT REQUIREMENT

Account required vs. guest checkout option

A: All users must create an account or log in before paying.
B: Guest checkout is available; account creation is offered after purchase.

First-purchase conversion +22% Account creation –3% (shifted to post-purchase)

3. Decision · Checkout – Version A

Multi-step checkout with account required

In Version A, customers complete a four-step checkout and must log in or create an account before entering delivery details.

Checkout flow · Version A

Four steps to complete the order

The baseline flow separates authentication from delivery details, which can increase friction for first-time users.

1

Cart

Review products and quantities.

2

Log in

Account is required before delivery details.

3

Address

Enter address and contact information.

4

Payment

Pay securely by card.

Customers must log in or create an account to complete the order in Version A.

3. Decision · Checkout – Version B

Streamlined checkout with guest option

Version B combines steps and adds a guest checkout option to reduce friction, especially for first-time customers arriving from campaigns.

Checkout flow · Version B

Three clear steps

The optimised flow groups related actions together, while keeping a clear sense of progress from basket to confirmation.

1

Cart & slot

Confirm basket and choose delivery window.

2

Details

Enter address and contact information.

3

Payment

Pay and receive real-time order updates.

Account requirement · Version B

Guest checkout and account benefits

The experiment compares a strict account requirement with a guest path where registration is proposed after payment.

Guest checkout

Complete the purchase without creating an account; only email and delivery details are required.

Log in or create account

Save addresses, favourite products and order history for future purchases.
